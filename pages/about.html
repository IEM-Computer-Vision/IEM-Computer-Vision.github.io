IEM CV Club

About Us

IEM-Computer-Vision Club(IEM-CV) is a community of students highly enthusiastic about computer vision. We are a team currently of 10 People. We also aim to organize various learning sessions for people new to the computer vision. We highly promote the idea of Contribution by Collaborations.
Feel free to contact any of our members any time in case of any queries!
Happy Learning!
